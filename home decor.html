Artificial Plants


Artificial flowers are imitations of natural flowering plants used for commercial or residential decoration. They are sometimes made for scientific purposes (the collection of glass flowers at Harvard University, for example, illustrates the flora of the United States)

Table Lamp


In addition to adding ambiance to your space, table lamps also provide task lighting for reading and work. Whether you're working on a project at your desk or reading a book in bed, a table lamp can provide the necessary lighting to help you see clearly and avoid eye strain.


Dining Table


Dining room layouts are configurations of furniture and space design aimed to optimize social interaction and functionality during meals. Central to the layout is the dining table, which can vary in shape—such as rectangular, round, or square—to fit the room and accommodate varying numbers of people
